About the Association

The objectives of the OCFA are:

  • To promote harmony and unity within the Ozi-Edem community.
  • To discover and develop football talent among youths.
  • To instill discipline, respect, and responsibility through sports.
  • To organize competitive and recreational football activities for community engagement.
